# Creative Strategy

## Overview
Why creative is now the primary lever practitioners control as bidding and targeting automate.

## Asset-first thinking
Designing for asset-based campaigns (RSAs, PMax asset groups) rather than fixed ads.

## Responsive Search Ads
Headline/description quantity, pinning sparingly, and feeding Ad Strength without gaming it.

## Asset groups for PMax
Theming asset groups, coverage across formats, and avoiding thin groups.

## Creative for the funnel
Matching message to intent stage across Search, Demand Gen, and Video.

## Messaging and offers
Value proposition, proof, and offer testing that maps to business goals.

## Visual and video assets
Image/video requirements, aspect ratios, and production-light ways to get coverage.

## Testing creative
Structured testing, what "winning" means with automation, and reading asset performance labels.

## Brand safety and compliance
Policy, claims, and brand consistency guardrails.

## Common mistakes
Thin asset coverage, over-pinning, untested offers, and ignoring asset reporting.
